The System Logic, Step by Step

A closed-loop allocation pipeline governed entirely by code. Every dollar movement maps to one of four auditable states.

  1. 01

    Baseline Anchor Deployment

    Upon capital intake, exactly 50% is routed into a low-cost S&P 500 index core. This anchor compounds passively and acts as the portfolio's structural gravity well.

    ALLOCATION → SPX_CORE = 0.50

  2. 02

    DCAX2 Surveillance Sweep

    The engine scans all 38 target equities on a continuous loop. When an asset's drawdown exceeds 3x its DCAX2 volatility band, a conditional buy signal is armed and validated against liquidity gates.

    IF DRAWDOWN ≥ 3.0 × DCAX2 → ARM_ENTRY()

  3. 03

    Operational Slot Activation

    Validated signals fill up to 10 concurrent operational slots, ranked by layer conviction within the Tech Stack Matrix. Slot capacity prevents over-concentration automatically.

    MAX_SLOTS = 10 · RANK_BY = LAYER_WEIGHT

  4. 04

    Milestone Trim & Capital Recycling

    As positions appreciate through milestone gates M1–M4, the Node trims predefined fractions and recycles proceeds into the baseline anchor — locking gains without exiting the thesis prematurely.

    AT MILESTONE_Mn → TRIM(fraction) → RECYCLE_TO_BASELINE
